FAQ

Membership

  • How do I become a member?

    You can become a member by signing up at our front desk or by using our website.

  • What types of membership do you offer?

    We offer a variety of membership options, including monthly, yearly, and pay-as-you-go. Add to that we have three diffrent membership bundles found in our pricing section.

  • How much does membership cost?

    Our membership fees vary depending on the type of membership you choose. Please contact us for more information.

Facilities

  • What equipment do you have?

    We have a wide variety of equipment, including cardio machines, weightlifting machines, and free weights.

  • Do you offer personal training?

    Yes, we have certified personal trainers available for one-on-one sessions or group training.

  • What are your hours of operation?

    We are open from 6am to 10pm Monday through Friday, and 8am to 8pm on weekends.

Policies

  • What is your cancellation policy?

    You may cancel your membership at any time, but please note that we requirec a 30 day notice before the cancellation takes place.

  • Do you have a dress code?

    We ask that our members wear appropriate athletic attire and get a towel with them while using our facilities.

  • Do you allow children?

    Children under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.
